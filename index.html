Frequently Asked Questions
Answering foundational inquiries regarding operational workflows
What services do you provide? +
We offer comprehensive monthly financial solutions tailored for small businesses, traders, and startups. Our services include end-to-end bookkeeping, routine Tally/Zoho/Excel data entry, bank reconciliation statement (BRS) preparation, customer & vendor account reconciliation, inventory tracking management, and structured monthly management information system (MIS) reporting.
Are you an affordable alternative to a full-time accountant? +
Yes, absolutely. Hiring a dedicated full-time accountant often demands a significant salary and infrastructure cost. We act as your outsourced corporate accounting department, delivering identical accuracy, systematic data structure, and timely statutory compliance at a fraction of the cost.
How do we share our financial files or bills with your team? +
Data sharing is simple and highly flexible. You can share digital scans of your invoices and bank statements via WhatsApp, cloud storage link, email, or schedule a periodic data collection route depending on your transaction complexity.
Do you assist businesses with old or backlogged data entry cleaning? +
Yes, we specialize in financial record cleanup. If your past data fields, accounts statements, or Tally books have backlog entry gaps, our accounting desk can process, sort, and reconstruct old files systematically into organized financial columns.
Is my business financial data kept fully safe and confidential? +
Data confidentiality is our primary priority. We handle all account entries on isolated system architectures with encrypted backups, ensuring none of your supplier indices, sales rates, or internal margins are shared or leaked.
