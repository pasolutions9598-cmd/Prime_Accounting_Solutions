Frequently Asked Questions
1. What accounting services do you provide? +
We offer comprehensive monthly financial solutions tailored for small businesses, traders, and startups. Our services include end-to-end bookkeeping, routine Tally/Zoho/Excel data entry, bank reconciliation statement (BRS) preparation, customer & vendor account reconciliation, inventory tracking management, and structured monthly management information system (MIS) reporting.
2. Are you an affordable alternative to a full-time accountant? +
Yes, absolutely. Hiring a dedicated full-time accountant often demands a significant salary and infrastructure cost. We act as your outsourced corporate accounting department, delivering identical accuracy, systematic data structure, and timely statutory compliance at a fraction of the cost.
3. How do you share financial dashboards with clients? +
We compile clean, high-resolution visual infographics and analytical sheets directly. They are dispatched via cloud sheets, encrypted mail summaries, or client dashboards so that you can view and monitor financial graphs cleanly on all responsive mobile or desktop screens.
4. Is my enterprise data secure and kept confidential? +
Data privacy is our supreme guideline. All your ledgers, tax preparation files, incoming invoices, and custom business MIS parameters are cataloged safely under strict internal processing protocols with redundant localized structural cloud backups.
5. Can you clean up backlogged or disordered account registers? +
Yes, we specialize in retrospective ledger management. Our team can rebuild data backlogs, spot discrepancies, audit old statements, settle entry errors, and perform deep historical cross-verifications to bring your active books 100% up to date.
