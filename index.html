Frequently Asked Questions
What services do you provide? +
We offer comprehensive monthly financial solutions tailored for small businesses, traders, and startups. Our services include end-to-end bookkeeping, routine Tally Prime data entry, bank reconciliation statement (BRS) preparation, customer & vendor account reconciliation, inventory tracking management, and structured monthly management information system (MIS) reporting. Additionally, we handle end-to-end GST data preparation (GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B filings) to keep your business fully compliant.
Are you an affordable alternative to a full-time accountant? +
Yes, absolutely. Hiring a dedicated full-time accountant often demands a significant salary and infrastructure cost. We act as your outsourced corporate accounting department, delivering identical accuracy, systematic data structure, and timely statutory compliance at a fraction of the cost. Our flexible monthly subscriptions are designed to easily fit the operational budgets of growing MSMEs.
How does your monthly subscription work? +
Our pricing model is completely transparent and structured into three tiers based on your business volume and operational requirements. The 'Basic' package is optimized for startups or service professionals needing fundamental data logging. The 'Standard' tier is designed for expanding trading businesses that require regular bank reconciliations and precise ledger oversight. The 'Premium' package offers comprehensive corporate-grade financial management, including detailed monthly management reports and dedicated data consultation.
How will I share my data with you? +
Data transmission is entirely streamlined for your convenience. You can securely share your raw purchase/sale invoices, bank statements, and relevant financial receipts via WhatsApp, email, or cloud shared folders on a weekly or monthly basis. Our team processes the documentation digitally, updates your books systematically, and provides clean reporting summaries without disrupting your daily operations.
Is my financial data secure with Prime Accounting Solutions? +
Data security and strict client confidentiality are the foundational principles of our firm. We implement robust access controls and isolated storage frameworks to ensure your corporate ledgers, turnover records, and banking transactions remain completely secure. Your information is strictly utilized for professional processing and compliance reporting, and is never disclosed to any third party under any circumstances.
